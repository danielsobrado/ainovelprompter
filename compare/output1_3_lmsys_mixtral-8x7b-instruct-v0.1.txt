Varian and Elric trudged towards the outpost, their limbs heavy with fatigue and worry. The sun hung low in the sky, casting long shadows that stretched out from the trees.

"Goblins have killed three cows in the frontier villages," a rider informed them as they approached the outpost. "But they didn't take the bodies."

Varian reported the recent incident in the village where a girl was attacked, and no attacker was found. Captain Aldric's face grew grave. "Increase patrols and search the borders," he ordered.
